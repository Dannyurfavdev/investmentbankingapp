Loans and Grants

Loans are funds given to a person or entity in exchange for future repayment of the loan value amount with interests, while providing a collateral. The recipient is expected to pay the interest incurred by the loan and also the principal amount borrowed. You can opt in for a loan to adequately fund a business setup or expansion. You can also apply for a personal loan to meet urgent/emergency situations.

However, grants, do not need to be repaid, but in applying for grants, you need to pass the eligibility criteria as well as demonstrate adequate knowledge, interests, and capability in the field for which the grant is to be allocated. Then there will be a shortlisting process in which the eventual recipient of the grant will be selected.

If you’re waiting to receive your first benefit payment and you’re in financial difficulty, you could apply for a short-term advance to tide you over. If you qualify, you’ll usually receive the loan the same day or the next working day. How much you receive will depend on your circumstances.
